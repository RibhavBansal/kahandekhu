# KahanDekhu — Play Store listing (ASO-optimized)

Paste-ready copy for Play Console. Keyword-optimized so you surface for "where to watch", "what to watch", "streaming", and platform names — not just the brand name. Includes a full **Hindi** localization.

> ASO rule of thumb: the **title** and **short description** carry the most search weight; the **long description** helps but never keyword-stuff (Google penalizes it). Installs + ratings + retention matter most — drive them via the WhatsApp bot, share cards, and social.

## ASO checklist

- Keep the **title** brand-first but with the key phrase ("Where to Watch") — best for both brand recall and search.
- The keywords you want to rank for appear naturally in the copy: *where to watch, what to watch, streaming, OTT, movie, web series, India, Netflix, Prime Video, JioHotstar, SonyLIV, ZEE5, watchlist, recommendations,* and regional languages.
- Add the **Hindi** translation — captures Hindi/Hinglish searches and is an easy edge for an India-first app.
- Screenshots with short captions ("Search any title", "See where to watch", "Filter by language") also feed ranking and conversion.
- **UI 2.0 (Jul 2026):** retake all store screenshots — lead with the **Top 10 in India** ranked rail and the spotlight home screen; they're the most striking frames. Also refresh `screenshot-mobile.png` / `screenshot-wide.png` in the repo (used by the PWA install sheet).
- Ratings + installs are the real ranking fuel → push people from the WhatsApp bot, share cards, Reddit/WhatsApp groups, and Instagram to install and rate.
